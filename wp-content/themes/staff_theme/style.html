Silver Birch Books are Here

January 7, 2013

All students in grade 6 have the opportunity to be part of the Ontario Library Association’s Forest of Reading program. With 30 books split evenly into 3 categories; fiction, non-fiction and express, there are sure to be books for everyone. Any student reading more than 5 in a category will be able to cast a ballot in a province wide vote at the end of April for a winner in each category. Tracking sheets are up in the library ready to keep count. Happy reading grade 6′s. For more information see:
